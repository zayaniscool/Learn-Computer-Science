Network Engineer:

Network Engineers are the architects of the digital highways that connect the modern world. They design, implement, and manage network infrastructure, ensuring seamless communication between devices and systems. These professionals deal with network protocols, security measures, and optimization strategies to guarantee reliable and efficient data transfer.

Compared to Software Architects, who design high-level software structures and define technical standards, Network Engineers concentrate on the physical and virtual connectivity of systems. While Software Architects ensure that software solutions align with business goals and technical specifications, Network Engineers ensure the uninterrupted flow of data.
