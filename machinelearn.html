Machine Learning:

Machine Learning Engineers are at the forefront of artificial intelligence and data-driven technologies. They specialize in developing and deploying machine learning models and algorithms. These professionals work on a wide array of tasks, from natural language processing and computer vision to recommendation systems. Their goal is to enable computers to learn from data and make predictions or decisions autonomously.

In contrast to Network Engineers, who design and manage network infrastructure, Machine Learning Engineers focus on data-driven applications. Network Engineers ensure the efficient communication of data between devices and systems, while Machine Learning Engineers are responsible for implementing advanced algorithms that enhance the intelligence and capabilities of applications.
