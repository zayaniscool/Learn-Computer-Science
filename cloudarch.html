Cloud Architect:

Cloud Architects are the visionaries behind the shift to cloud computing. They design and manage cloud infrastructure, determining the best deployment models and integration strategies for businesses. These professionals ensure that organizations can harness the power of the cloud to scale, innovate, and thrive in the digital age.

Contrasting with DevOps Engineers, who emphasize the seamless collaboration between development and IT operations, Cloud Architects concentrate on the technical aspects of cloud infrastructure. DevOps Engineers streamline development processes, while Cloud Architects optimize the cloud solutions that underpin those processes.
