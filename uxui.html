UX/UI Engineer:

UX/UI Designers are the artists of the digital world. They craft user-friendly and visually appealing interfaces for digital products, ensuring that users have a delightful and intuitive experience. These professionals delve into user research, information architecture, and interactive design to create interfaces that captivate and engage users.

Unlike Machine Learning Engineers, who build and deploy machine learning models, UX/UI Designers focus on aesthetics and user interaction. Machine Learning Engineers, on the other hand, are concerned with implementing advanced algorithms for data analysis, shaping the intelligence that powers applications.
