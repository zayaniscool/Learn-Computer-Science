Software Architect:

Software Architects are the masterminds behind complex software systems. They design the high-level structure of software applications, determining the arrangement of components, interactions, and technical standards. These professionals provide a blueprint for development teams and ensure that the overall system is robust, scalable, and aligns with organizational objectives.

In contrast to IT Project Managers, who oversee the execution of IT projects, Software Architects primarily focus on the technical aspects of software design. IT Project Managers manage project timelines, budgets, and resources, ensuring that projects are delivered successfully. While Project Managers focus on project management, Software Architects are deeply rooted in technical design and decision-making.
