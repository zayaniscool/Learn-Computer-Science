IT Project Manager

IT Project Managers are the orchestrators of technology initiatives within organizations. They oversee the planning, execution, and monitoring of IT projects, from software development to infrastructure upgrades. These professionals coordinate teams, allocate resources, and ensure that project goals are met within defined timelines and budgets.

Comparatively, Technical Support Engineers provide technical assistance and troubleshoot issues for end-users and organizations. They play a crucial role in resolving technical problems and ensuring smooth system operation. IT Project Managers manage the entire project lifecycle, from initiation to closure, while Technical Support Engineers are dedicated to technical problem-solving.
